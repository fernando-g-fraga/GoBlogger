# Must-Have Items for Cat Owners

## 1. Litter Box
A clean and accessible litter box is essential for your cat's hygiene.

## 2. Cat Food
High-quality cat food that meets your cat's nutritional needs.

## 3. Scratching Post
A scratching post helps keep your cat's claws healthy and prevents damage to furniture.

## 4. Toys
Interactive toys to keep your cat entertained and active.

## 5. Cat Bed
A comfortable bed where your cat can rest and sleep.
